Project Overview

1. Project Objective

This project focuses on analyzing the bank’s capital mobilization, providing insights into deposit performance, cost of funds, and financial stability indicators. Through Gold-tier data, information is refined, aggregated, and prepared for visualization in a dashboard to support strategic decision-making.

2. Data Structure

- Data Schema:

- Table:

+ FACT_FUND: Central fact table storing deposit balances, interest rates, and funding costs on a daily snapshot basis.

+ DIM_ACCOUNT : Classification of deposits by product type (CASA, Term Deposits, Interbank, Securities).

+ DIM_TERM & DIM_REMAINING_TERM: Categorization of deposits by original and remaining tenure.

+ BANK_CAPITAL: Captures the bank’s capital fund, supporting calculations for CAR and financial safety metrics.

3. Dashboard

- Metrics:

+ Total Deposits: Aggregated deposit balance across all accounts, tracked daily and compared YoY.

+ COF (Cost of Funds): Weighted cost of capital mobilization, critical for determining profitability.

+ CASA Ratio – Percentage of total deposits coming from Current Account Savings Account (CASA), influencing cost efficiency.

- Report Structure & Visualizations: The dashboard includes multiple analytical views:

+ Year-over-Year (YoY) Comparison – Trend charts displaying deposit fluctuations against the previous year.

+ CASA Ratio & Deposit Trends – Line and clustered column chart tracking the share of CASA in total deposits over time.

+ Fund by Term & Remaining Term – Column chart compares total fund by tenure buckets.
